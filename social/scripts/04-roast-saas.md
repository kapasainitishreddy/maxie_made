## Why this works
Contrarian takes drive comments at 3–5x the rate of positive content because disagreement is a higher-arousal action than agreement. Calling out a "common enemy" (shallow SaaS) creates an in-group of viewers who feel seen. The creator positions themselves as both critic and practitioner ("I ship five products alone"), which defuses the "hater" label and earns trust. The CTA is comment-bait, not a sell — the algorithm rewards the comment velocity.
