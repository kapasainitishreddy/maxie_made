## Why this works
Pattern interrupts exploit the first-3-second skip reflex — a counterintuitive claim ("5 SaaS, 1 dev") forces the thumb to stop. The rapid product montage in the middle provides visual proof density (5 logos in 5 seconds = high information-per-frame), which boosts watch time and triggers TikTok's "rewatch" signal for replay value.
