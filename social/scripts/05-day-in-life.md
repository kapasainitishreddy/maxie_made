**Pattern:** Day-in-the-life (POV → chronological slice → emotional beat)  
**Hook (first 3s):** "POV: you run five production SaaS apps and you are the entire company."

## Why this works
Day-in-the-life content is the #1 "save" category on TikTok and IG Reels for B2B/tech audiences because it compresses a whole workday into a digestible 45-second montage. The chronological structure (timestamps on screen) gives the viewer a sense of progression, which boosts completion rate. The contrast "5 apps / 0 employees" is the emotional hook — viewers stay to see how one person actually does it.
