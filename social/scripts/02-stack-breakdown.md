## Why this works
Numbered lists create "open loops" — viewers stay to hear #5 because leaving mid-list feels like missing a payoff. The counted hook ("Five things") sets a clear finish line, which the algorithm rewards with higher average view duration. The "kill the dead weight" point is the most controversial item, designed to drive comments and saves.
